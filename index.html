Tucson is an innovative, sustainable community that is a model of desert-adaptive living and a hub for ecotourism - a gateway to the Sky Islands with their diverse habitats and wildlife.

There is more to Tucson than just sun (though we have plenty!) We are well known for our outdoor activities, but Tucson also boasts a thriving art and food scene. Tucson was designated a City of Gastronomy - not just because of our great Mexican food - but because Tucson has the longest continuous agriculture in North America. Our chefs are inspired by our diverse cultures, local sourcing and native desert foods.
